Lexis Sunshine Coast

Queensland's coast, one relaxed campus at a time

The Sunshine Coast stretches from quiet beach suburbs to buzzing coastal strips, with a laid-back, outdoorsy lifestyle that's easy to fall into.

Why Sunshine Coast?

Students here get the best of both worlds — real Aussie beach culture right outside the classroom, plus quick access to Brisbane and the green hinterland behind the coast whenever you want a change of scene.

The campus community is friendly and close-knit, with a steady rhythm of weekly activities that make it easy to build friendships fast, alongside your studies.

🌿 Hinterland day trips
