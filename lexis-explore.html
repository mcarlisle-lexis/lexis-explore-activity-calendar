Learning English is only half the story

Beyond the classroom, every Lexis campus runs a full calendar of weekly activities and monthly adventures — surf lessons, wildlife encounters, city tours and more — so students build confidence in English while actually living it.

Life outside the classroom

English sticks best when it's used, not just studied. That's why every Lexis campus pairs its courses with a packed program of free weekly activities — conversation clubs, sport, cooking, arts and social nights — plus bigger monthly trips run through Lexis Travel Discovery.

The calendar further down this page pulls activities from all six campuses in one place. Filter by campus, browse by week or month, and click into anything that catches your eye for the full details.

Regular academic activities

Free, drop-in support that runs every week at every campus — no booking, no pressure, just turn up.
