Lexis Explore

Learning English is only half the story

Beyond the classroom, every Lexis campus runs a full calendar of weekly activities and monthly adventures — surf lessons, wildlife encounters, city tours and more — so students build confidence in English while actually living it.

Serious Study, Serious Fun

Every day at Lexis includes one activity — a mix of academic workshops and out-and-about sessions that get you into the destination itself, not stuck behind a desk. From grammar clubs to surf lessons, each one is built to sharpen your English while you explore alongside classmates from all over the world.

We also make a point of bringing everyone together regularly: international lunches where you'll taste dishes from a dozen different countries, sessions dedicated to classic Australian food, and our famous termly sausage sizzle to send the term off in style.

Lexis Academic Clubs

Free, drop-in support that runs every week at every campus — no booking, no pressure, just turn up.
